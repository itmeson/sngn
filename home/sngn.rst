Strega Nona
###########

:date: 2014-07-15
:tags: growth, units 
:category: book


**Strega Nona**, by Tomie de Paola, is a delightful version of the Sorcerer's Apprentice tale, told notably by Goethe and in the Disney movie **Fantasia**.  Magic is real and dangerous if not used by the wise.
